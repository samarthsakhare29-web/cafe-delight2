Beyond the Bean

Learn about the passion, people, and processes that structure the Delight experience.

The Delight Philosophy

We believe that coffee is more than just a beverage; it's a bridge between communities, a catalyst for conversation, and a daily ritual that deserves utmost respect.

Sourcing our beans from fair-trade farms across Ethiopia, Colombia, and Guatemala, we ensure that every cup poured here honors the hands that cultivated it.

  • 100% Ethically Sourced
  • Micro-Batch Roasted
  • Community Focused

Our Journey

From a small cart to your favorite neighborhood retreat.

2018

The Humble Cart

Delight begins as a humble three-wheeled coffee cart navigating the busy downtown streets, serving our signature roast to morning commuters.

2020

The First Brick & Mortar

We opened our doors in Chinchwad, transitioning from wheels to walls, but keeping the intimate, friendly vibe.

2023

Expanding the Menu

Partnering with local artisanal bakers, we introduced our full menu of pastries, toasts, and brunch items to complement our coffee.

Today

Delight Roasters

We launched our own small-batch roasting facility, allowing us complete control over the flavor profile from bean to cup.

Meet Elena

Founder & Head Roaster

"I wanted to create a space that felt like an extension of your own living room, but with much better coffee."

Elena traveled through South America studying local roasting techniques before returning home to establish Delight. Her vision was simple: eliminate the pretension often associated with specialty coffee, focusing instead on accessibility and warmth.
